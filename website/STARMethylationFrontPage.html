Strand Detection determines if the bisulfite converted sequence is a PCR product or representative of the BSC sequence itself. If the Strand Detection algorithm detects that the sequence is a PCR product, it will use the reverse complement of the sequence when mapping to the genome. Since some sequences are reverse complemented before mapping, only total mismatch options are allowed. If you do not used Strand Detection, none of the input sequences are not reverse complemented and you may thusly specify error thresholds for the typically less reliable sequences at the 3' end.
